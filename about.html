About CampusBuzz

Our mission is to digitally connect campus communities, enabling students to discover events, opportunities and each other — in a single platform.

Mission

To make campus life more accessible and vibrant by giving students tools to share news, manage events, find internships and collaborate.

Vision

Every campus is connected, informed and thriving — students lead the culture and opportunities flow transparently.

Goals

  • Provide timely, relevant campus information.
  • Enable simple event organization and discovery.
  • Make internship/job leads visible to students.

How CampusBuzz started

The idea emerged in 2024 when founders noticed fragmented communication across clubs, departments and students. A prototype launched at one college and quickly grew through word of mouth as students used it to coordinate events and share opportunities.

Today, CampusBuzz continues to build features driven by student feedback.
